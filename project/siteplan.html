Mubbala Phillip - Website Site Plan

Site Name

Mwanaweika Hotel

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Mwanaweika Hotel website is to provide an engaging and user-friendly platform for potential guests to explore the hotel’s offerings, amenities, and services. The site will serve as a digital brochure that enhances the hotel’s online presence and improves customer experience by offering essential information in a visually appealing and accessible manner.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a room at the Mwanaweika Hotel?
  • What services and amenities are available at the hotel for guests?
